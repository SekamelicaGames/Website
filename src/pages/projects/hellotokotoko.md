Hello Tokotoko! is an idle game in the same world as Tokotoko, featuring reoccuring characters and cute and goofy Tokotokos! You manage the Cozynest, a floating island where Tokotoko comes to rest. Decorate the cozynest with varied furnitures to attract the Tokotokos, meet them, and photograph them with Oko! Find the secrets interactions with the Tokotokos, their preferences, all the furnitures and surprising photos to uncover!
